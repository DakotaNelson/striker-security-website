Pushpin-web

See social media in the real world

Available on Github!

People are talking - are you listening?

Social media permeates modern society. Whether it's customers, competitors, or criminals, they're broadcasting to the world - and to you, if you listen.

Pushpin-web provides a simple and intuitive interface to keep an eye on social media around your real-world assets. While other platforms focus on marketing or branding with keywords, pushpin-web keeps its focus on the physical world by collecting and displaying geotagged media near critical locations you designate.

Striker Security built pushpin-web from the ground up on behalf of Black Hills Information Security, the project's sponsor. Pushpin-web is open source software released under a permissive MIT license. Striker's customers can receive a private deployed instance, training, and development on the core open-source software or bespoke private modifications - contact us today to get started.
