Sneaky-Creeper Presented at BSides Las Vegas

What a great time Dakota Nelson
July 8, 2016

Last year, several contributors to sneaky-creeper and I submitted to BSides Las Vegas - and were accepted! This was my first national-level conference talk, and I couldn't have ended up at a better place.

Sneaky-creeper is a data exfiltration framework that uses third-party domains to transfer information across the internet. Instead of standing up your own servers - which is a lot of work and will likely stand out in domain reputation rankings, making it an easy target for blacklists - simply take advantage of all the places you can store data on better-known cloud services. Whether it's uploading to dropbox, saving data in Salesforce, or embedding bits in audio files uploaded to Soundcloud, there are an unlimited number of reputable places to relay your data through.
