What We Do

Striker provides R&D and operations support for advanced cyber intelligence, surveillance, and reconnaissance programs.

Hello, world

Whether you're leading a top-notch cyber ISR team, or just getting started building out your capabilities, Striker can help. Our team gathers data - offensive, defensive, or OSINT - and builds systems, whether custom or off-the-shelf, to advance and support any program.

Most importantly, Striker focuses on people. Too many technologies aim to replace humans, or simply don't seem to be designed for humans at all. That's why Striker seeks to truly understand the people and the problems we're called in to solve, rather than just picking from a list of shiny new toys to deploy.

We understand that code is cost - not just to create, but to maintain. We aim to thoughtfully automate and enhance your existing expertise, not overload you with more "solutions."

Get in touch

If you've got problems, we've got ideas - and the expertise to make them a reality. We'll work with you to determine the best approach for your specific situation. All communications are strictly confidential.
